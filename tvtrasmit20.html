TV Transmitter

Introduction

A TV transmitter is a device that transmits television signals to receivers, such as televisions. It takes the video and audio signals and sends them via electromagnetic waves.

How It Works

The TV transmitter converts video and audio signals into radio frequency (RF) signals. These RF signals are then transmitted through antennas to TV receivers in homes, where they are converted back into audio and visual content.

Components

  • Antenna
  • Modulator
  • Amplifier
  • Oscillator

Technical Specifications

Parameter Value
